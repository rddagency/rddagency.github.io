Our Story

Built in the
Trenches.

RDDA did not begin in a boardroom or a venture capital pitch. It began in 2016 when M. Razi, a former intelligence advisor who had spent 18 years in adversarial cyber operations, watched a Fortune 500 client suffer a catastrophic breach that should have been prevented. The incident was not the result of a lack of technology — it was the result of a lack of operators who truly understood how adversaries think and move.

"The industry was full of tools and dashboards.
What was missing was judgment."

Razi founded RDDA with a single principle: hire operators, not salespeople. Build every team the way a tier-one special operations unit builds itself — with specialists, clear roles, and a culture of brutal honesty about what works and what does not.

Today, RDDA is the trusted cybersecurity partner for governments, central banks, critical infrastructure operators, and the world's largest technology companies. Our clients include entities that cannot afford a single security failure — and they trust us precisely because we hold ourselves to the same standard.

What We Stand For

Core Values

These are not words on a wall. They are the criteria by which we hire, promote, and evaluate every person at RDDA — from our founder to our most recently joined analyst.

01
Operator Mindset
We think like the adversaries we defend against. Curiosity, persistence, and a refusal to accept "probably secure" are not personality traits here — they are professional requirements. We hire people who have genuinely operated in adversarial environments and who cannot switch that instinct off.
02
Brutal Honesty
We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. If your environment has a critical exposure, you will know it — not softened in consultant language but stated clearly with evidence. Internally, we hold each other to the same standard. Comfortable lies destroy security postures and careers.
03
Relentless Precision
Security is a domain where imprecision has consequences. Every finding is verified. Every report is technically accurate. Every remediation recommendation is tested in our lab before it reaches a client. We do not estimate, guess, or round. We confirm.
04
Client First, Always
Our clients trust us with their most sensitive systems and secrets. That trust is a weight we carry seriously. We never prioritise our commercial interests over a client's security interests. If we cannot help, we say so. If another provider is better suited, we say that too.
05
Continuous Mastery
Adversary tradecraft evolves every day. RDDA invests more per analyst in training, research, and tool development than any firm in our sector. Every analyst has an annual training budget of $12,000. We run internal red team exercises, CTFs, and research sprints year-round. Stagnation is a security risk.
06
Ethical Conduct
We operate with the highest ethical standards in a field where the line between defensive and offensive capability is thin. We work only within authorised scopes, we protect the data we access, and we refuse engagements that we believe are intended to cause harm. Ethics are not negotiable — not for any client, at any price.
